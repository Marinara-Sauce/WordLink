WordLink

Try to get from the starting word to the target word in as little number of steps as possible. To make a step, change one letter in the previous word to create a new word, with that word being a valid word. Do this until you get to the target word. At any time you can click the "Go Back to Here" button next to a word to reset the puzzle back to that step. There are many ways to solve these. Compete with your friends to see who can get the lowest number of steps. Check back tomorrow for a new puzzle and to see the best possible solution for the previous puzzle.
