The goal of WordLink is to get from the starting word to the target word taking as little number of "steps" as possible.

Steps

A "step" is when you change a single letter from the previous word, resulting in a new word. This word must still be a valid word in the dictionary.

For example, say you need to step from the word fast. Some steps from fast are:

  • past
  • fact
  • fest

For each puzzle each step proceeds the previous word. Your goal is to get to the target word.

Example Puzzle

For example, say the starting word is harp and the target word is ramp. A solution to this puzzle is as follows:

  • harp
  • carp
  • camp
  • ramp

This is the best solution to this puzzle, taking 4 steps.

Playing the Game

When you launch the game you'll see the current puzzle, with the starting word at the top and the bottom word at the bottom. Between them is where you'll enter each step. Use the keyboard at the bottom of the screen to type the word, then hit the "enter" button on the left next to the Z key. After entering a valid step (one letter was changed and it's a valid english word), the word will appear in the list. Keep writing steps until you reach the target.

If at any time you want to go back to a previous step, simply tap that word and the puzzle will reset back to that state.

A new puzzle is created daily at midnight (EST). Note that your solution may not be the best solution. Try to refine it to use the smallest number of steps possible.

After the next puzzle is released, come back to view the solution for the previous puzzle as well as statistics on how everyone else did.

Good luck, and thanks for playing!
